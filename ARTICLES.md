ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

of

ANONYMOUS ART LTD

(Private company limited by shares)


---

1. INTERPRETATION

1.1 In these Articles, unless the context otherwise requires:

“the Act” means the Companies Act 2006.

“the Company” means Anonymous Art Ltd.

“Director” means a director of the Company.

“Share” means an ordinary share in the capital of the Company.

“Shareholder” means a holder of a Share whose name is entered in the register of members.

“Active Edition” means a Share that confers voting and participation rights under Article 7.

“Residual Edition” means a Share that confers ownership only and no voting rights.

“Certificate” means a physical share certificate issued by the Company, which may also constitute an artwork.

“Supermajority” means approval by not less than seventy-five per cent (75%) of the voting rights of Active Edition shareholders present and voting.


1.2 References to statutes include any statutory modification or re-enactment.


---

2. COMPANY OBJECTS

2.1 The Company is formed to operate as an artistic, cultural, and experimental entity.

2.2 The Company may create, issue, hold, acquire, dispose of, and manage physical, digital, and hybrid artworks, assets, and systems.

2.3 The Company has full capacity to carry on or undertake any lawful activity.


---

3. SHARE CAPITAL

3.1 The share capital of the Company consists of 21 ordinary shares of £1,000,000 nominal value each.

3.2 No Share shall be issued other than as an ordinary share.

3.3 The Company shall not allot or issue additional shares except with the approval of a Supermajority of Shareholders and an amendment to these Articles.


---

4. PARTLY PAID SHARES

4.1 Each Share is issued as partly paid.

4.2 The amount paid on issue of each Share shall be £1.

4.3 No interest shall be payable on any unpaid share capital.


---

5. PROHIBITION ON CALLS

5.1 The Directors shall have no authority to make calls on unpaid share capital.

5.2 No call may be made unless:

approved by a Supermajority of Shareholders; and

this Article is first amended by special resolution.



---

6. SHARE CERTIFICATES

6.1 The Company shall issue a share certificate to each Shareholder.

6.2 Certificates may be issued in physical form and may be treated as artworks.

6.3 The loss, destruction, or damage of a Certificate does not affect ownership of the Share.

6.4 Replacement certificates may be issued at the discretion of the Directors.


---

7. EDITIONS AND PARTICIPATION

7.1 All Shares are ordinary shares, but participation rights are subject to edition status.

7.2 A Share shall be designated as one of the following:

Active Edition — conferring voting and participation rights;

Residual Edition — conferring ownership without voting rights.


7.3 An Active Edition exists for a period of four (4) years from issue or renewal.

7.4 A Shareholder may elect to renew an Active Edition upon expiry.

7.5 If no renewal is elected, the Share automatically converts to a Residual Edition.

7.6 Ownership of a Share does not lapse by reason of edition status.


---

8. VOTING RIGHTS

8.1 Each Active Edition Share carries one vote.

8.2 Residual Edition Shares carry no voting rights.

8.3 Voting thresholds are as follows:

Ordinary resolutions: simple majority

Reserved matters: Supermajority


8.4 Reserved matters include:

amendment of these Articles

issuance of shares

removal of the Director

dissolution of the Company

disposal of core assets



---

9. TRANSFER OF SHARES

9.1 Shares may be transferred in accordance with the Act.

9.2 The Directors may refuse to register a transfer if permitted by law.

9.3 Transfer of a Share does not guarantee transfer of any Certificate.


---

10. DIRECTORS

10.1 The Company shall have at least one Director.

10.2 Directors are appointed and removed by Shareholders.

10.3 The Directors manage the Company’s business subject to these Articles.


---

11. ASSETS AND TREASURY

11.1 The Company may hold assets including but not limited to:

digital assets

cryptographic keys

Bitcoin and similar instruments

domain names

intellectual property


11.2 The method of custody is at the Director’s discretion.

11.3 Loss of access to an asset does not invalidate ownership.


---

12. RECORDS AND REGISTERS

12.1 The Company shall maintain statutory registers as required by law.

12.2 Records may be maintained digitally.

12.3 Access may be restricted to Shareholders.


---

13. DIVIDENDS

13.1 The Company is not obliged to declare dividends.

13.2 No expectation of profit or return is implied.


---

14. DISSOLUTION

14.1 The Company may be wound up only by Supermajority resolution.

14.2 Assets shall be distributed in accordance with the Act.


---

15. GENERAL

15.1 These Articles supersede any previous Articles.

15.2 If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in force.


---

END OF ARTICLES
