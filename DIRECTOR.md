# DIRECTOR - AA Ltd

Director - also known as The King

## Position within AA

The Director is the sole executive officer of Anonymous Art Ltd.

The role exists to legally represent the company and act on its behalf. There is one Director at any time.


---

## Core Responsibilities

The Director is responsible for all statutory and administrative duties, including:

maintaining statutory registers

filing required documents with Companies House

ensuring compliance with company law

managing company assets, including digital and physical holdings


The Director executes decisions approved by shareholders.


---

## Role within the Artwork

The Director functions as the human interface of Anonymous Art.

They are the visible executor of actions that give form to the work, including exhibitions, releases, and public-facing activities. The role itself is part of the AA system and may be referenced as such within artworks.


---

## Governance & Power

The Director holds executive authority but is subordinate to the shareholders.

They act within the limits defined by the Articles of Association and are accountable to the shareholders, who may remove or replace the Director in accordance with company law.


---

## Visibility & Anonymity

The Director may be public or pseudonymous.

Visibility is functional, not performative. The Director represents the company when required but is not positioned as the author of the work.


---

## Term & Commitment

The Director serves at the discretion of the shareholders.

There is no fixed term. Appointment and removal are governed by shareholder vote and statutory procedure.


---

## Ideal Director Profile

An ideal Director:

understands legal and fiduciary responsibility

is comfortable operating within defined constraints

can act decisively without personal authorship

respects the separation between governance and spectacle



---

## Function in the System

The Director enables Anonymous Art to operate within the legal world.

They translate structure into action and ensure the system remains functional, compliant, and intact.

The Director does not own the system.
They hold it together.
