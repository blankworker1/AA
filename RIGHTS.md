

## AA Ltd — 21 Shareholder 'The Chosen' Rights 

1. Right to Ownership: Legally own 1 of 21 shares in AA Ltd.


2. Right to Physical Artifact: Receive the numbered Prospectus, COA, and Share Certificate.


3. Right to NFC Verification: Verify provenance through embedded NFC tag linked to IPFS.


4. Right to Participate: Choose whether and when to engage with AA digital systems (dashboard, tipping, etc.).


5. Right to Anonymity: Maintain anonymity publicly while legally recognized as a shareholder.


6. Right to Influence: Vote on matters requiring shareholder approval (supermajority issues).


7. Right to Digital Access: Access digital Prospectus, IPFS records, and dashboard insights.


8. Right to Resale: Transfer ownership of share/artifact according to Articles.


9. Right to Collectability: Each share/artifact is a verifiably scarce object.


10. Right to Historical Record: Part of AA system provenance; your participation is permanently recorded.


11. Right to Engagement Ritual: Participate in LN signaling, tipping, or other structured AA interactions.


12. Right to Exclusive Updates: Receive communications about new AA artworks or editions.


13. Right to Authentication: Certify and demonstrate authenticity of all artifacts in your possession.


14. Right to Physical Exhibition: Loan your artifacts for gallery display if desired.


15. Right to Future Editions: Priority / visibility in subsequent AA editions or related projects.


16. Right to Intellectual Respect: Ownership does not imply rights to ideas, but to the artifact as intended by AA.


17. Right to Education: Access AA materials explaining system, philosophy, and provenance.


18. Right to Participate in Ritual: Be included in curated AA events or “art performances.”


19. Right to Digital Permanence: Artifacts and COA maintained via IPFS for long-term existence.


20. Right to Recognition: Membership in the network of The Chosen, a permanent “passport” status.


21. Right to Choice: Opt in or out of future AA projects, tipping systems, or interactions — full sovereignty.

Rights define the experiential, participatory, and artistic privileges of owners.
