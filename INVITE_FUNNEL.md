# Invite Funnel 

Anonymous Art Prospectus Invitation Funnel

## 1. Entry Point: QR Code

Placed in gallery, online, or social channels

Leads directly to Prospectus S (short version)

Free, low-cost, teaser content

Introduces AA philosophy and signals the start of conscious participation



---

## 2. Prospectus S (Short Version)

First 5–7 pages of the full Prospectus

Ends with a subtle prompt:

> “To access the full Prospectus, signal your interest with a Lightning payment.”



Optional LN payment: 1 satoshi (minimal signal, permissionless)



---

## 3. Prospectus L (Full Version)

21-page digital Prospectus

Includes AA system explanation, artworks, funnel mechanics

No mention of COA yet — preserves separate collectible experience

Delivered via Lightning message automatically after the first LN payment

PDF contains teaser for Invitation to Participate but no actionable invoice yet



---

## 4. Invitation to Participate

Delivered after Prospectus L

Purpose: Explain costs and mechanics to become a shareholder

Content Highlights:

Legal requirement: £1 to be a shareholder

Production cost for physical artifacts: £1,000

Total: £1,001 per share

Reinforces conscious choice, rarity, and exclusivity

Stacked provenance context: each physical artifact will be numbered


LN invoice QR at bottom: easy one-tap payment to claim share and receive physical Prospectus + COA + Share Certificate



---

## 5. Post-Payment

Upon LN payment completion:

Physical Prospectus shipped

COA included

Share certificate issued

NFC tag embedded, linking to IPFS-hosted digital COA


Participant is now one of The Chosen (21 shareholders)



---

## 6. Key Advantages

Tightly controlled funnel: low friction, low cost, full automation possible

Maintains AA philosophy: voluntary, conscious, sovereign participation

Stacked provenance reinforced: digital signal → LN payment → physical artifact → COA → NFC verification

Global, scalable, and low-risk: only 21 physical items need production

Engagement as ritual: each step is an artistic gesture
